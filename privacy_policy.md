AVIVO.AI PRIVACY POLICY

Avivo.ai is committed to safeguarding user data and ensuring responsible
AI practices across all products and services.

1. Data Collection — Minimum necessary information only.
2. Data Storage — No messages stored by default; encrypted if enabled.
3. Data Usage — Product improvement, debugging, reliability.
4. Third-Party Access — Secure encrypted transfer to trusted model APIs.
5. User Rights — Request deletion, export, or access anytime.
