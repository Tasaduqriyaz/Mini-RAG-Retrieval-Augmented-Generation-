# Avivo.ai — Company Overview

Avivo.ai is an AI-driven solutions company focused on building intelligent assistants,
workflow automation tools, and enterprise-ready generative AI applications.

## Our Mission
To simplify complex business processes using practical, reliable, and safe
AI systems that enhance human productivity.

## What We Do
- Develop AI Assistants for sales, HR, and operations  
- Build RAG-based knowledge systems using internal documents  
- Offer multi-agent automation workflows  
- Integrate computer vision and text-processing pipelines  
- Provide enterprise deployment & monitoring support  

## Core Values
1. Accuracy — Every answer should be grounded in real data.  
2. Reliability — Always deliver consistent behavior.  
3. Transparency — Explain decisions when possible.  
4. Privacy — User data must always be protected.
